Mah Jongg Players Companion Patterns

  1. Three Philosophers: Chow in each suit, Mixed Chow, Pair any suit

  2. Triple Knitting: 4 sets of each suit of same number, Pair of numbers different suit

  3. Moon at Bottom of Well: Four Chows and a Pair, all in Dots

  4. The Professors: Three mixed chows, One each Dragon, pair Winds

  5. Windy Chows: Chow each suit, One each Wind, another single any Wind

  6. Big Robert: Three runs of 4 tiles, one in each suit, Pair Winds or Dragons

  7. Hovering Angel: Chow in each suit, Pung Wind, Pair Dragon

  8. Little Brother: Chow in each suit, chow any suit, pair Winds

  9. A Run, A Pung, A Pair: One suit, run 1-9, pung, pair
