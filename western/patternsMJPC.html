Mah Jongg Players Companion Patterns

  1. A Run, A Pung, A Pair: One suit, run 1-9, pung, pair

  2. Big Robert: Three runs of 4 tiles, one in each suit, Pair Winds or Dragons

  3. Chow Chow: 4 Chows and a Pair, all in 1 suit

  4. Gates Hands

    Confused Gates: 111 in 1st suit, 999 in 2nd suit, 2-8 in 3rd suit with a tile paired

    Gates of Heaven: One suit, 111, 2-8 sequence with a tile paired, 999

    Golden Gates: One suit 22 44 66 88 pairs, pung 111 or 999, pung corresponding Dragon

  5. Grand Sequence: A 1-9 run, pair any of the run, pair another of the run, Pung Winds/Dragons

  6. Hovering Angel: Chow in each suit, Pung Wind, Pair Dragon

  7. Little Brother: Chow in each suit, Chow any suit, pair Winds

  8. Little Robert: Chow in each suit, Pung any suit, pair any suit

  9. Moon at Bottom of Well: Four Chows and a Pair, all in Dots

  10. The Professors: Three mixed Chows, One each Dragon, pair Winds

  11. Three Philosophers: Chow in each suit, Mixed Chow, Pair any suit

  12. Triple Knitting: 4 like numbers with a number in each suit, Pair of numbers with different suit

  13. Windy Chows: Chow each suit, One each Wind, another single any Wind
