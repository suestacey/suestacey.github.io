Wright Patterson Patterns

  1. Apple Blossom: 3 Mixed Chows, Pung of White, Pair Green

  2. A Hash: A pung in each suit, any number, Pair and Pung of Opposite Honors

  3. Little, Middle, Big Chows

    Little Chows: 123Chow in each Suit, 123 Mixed Chows, pair of 4s

    Middle Chows: 456Chow in each Suit, 456 Mixed Chows, pair of 3s

    Big Chows: 789Chow in each Suit, 789 Mixed Chows, pair of 6s

  4. Idiots: 4 like numbers with a number in each suit (no duplicates), Pair of Winds/Dragons

  5. Little 1-5-7-9: A pung of 1,5,7,9, Pair of 1 or 9

  6. Little 2-4-6-8: A pung of 2,4,6,8, Pair of 2 or 8

  7. Susie Q: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung South, Pair Green
