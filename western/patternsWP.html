Wright Patterson Patterns

  1. Apple Blossom: 3 Mixed Chows, Pung of White, Pair Green

  2. A Hash: A pung in each suit, any number, Pair and Pung of Opposite Honors

    Eleanor Q: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung East, Pair Red

    Neutrality: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung North, Pair 5 Bam

    Susie Q: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung South, Pair Green

    Wally: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung West, Pair White

  3. Chop Suey: Chows of 1-2-3 in each suit with NEWS and one Wind paired

  4. Chow Mein: Chows of 7-8-9 in each suit with NEWS and one Wind paired

  5. Doublets:

  6. Gates Hands

    Dragon Gates: One suit, 111 or 999 with 2-8 sequence (one tile paired), pung corresponding dragon

    Little Gates: Sequence 1-9, with a pair in 2-8 sequence, 19 in the other 2 suits

    Open Gates: 111 and sequence 2-5 in one suit, 5-8 and 999 in a different suit

    Windy Gates: 111 with sequence of 2-9 or 1-8 sequence with 999, Pung corresponding dragon

  7. Idiots: 4 like numbers with a number in each suit (no duplicates), Pair of Winds/Dragons

  8. Jewels Hands

    Emerald: 3,4,5,7,9,Green with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

    Garnet: 3,4,5,7,9,Red with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

    Jade: 2,3,4,6,8,Green with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

    Opal: 3,4,5,7,9,White with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

    Pearl: 2,3,4,6,8,White with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

    Ruby: 2,3,4,6,8,Red with 4 pungs and/or chows and a pair

  9. Little, Middle, Big Chows

    Little Chows: 123 Chow in each Suit, 123 Mixed Chows, pair of 4s

    Middle Chows: 456 Chow in each Suit, 456 Mixed Chows, pair of 3s

    Big Chows: 789 Chow in each Suit, 789 Mixed Chows, pair of 6s

  10. Little 1-5-7-9: A pung of 1,5,7,9, Pair of 1 or 9

  11. Little 2-4-6-8: A pung of 2,4,6,8, Pair of 2 or 8

  12. Ohio Snow Suit: A run of 1-9, pair any one from 2-8, SNOW
