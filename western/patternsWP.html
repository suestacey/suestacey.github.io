Wright Patterson Patterns

  1. Apple Blossom: 3 Mixed Chows, Pung of White, Pair Green

  2. Susie Q: 3 pungs of different numbers, one each suit, Pung South, Pair Green

  3. Little 1-5-7-9: A pung of 1,5,7,9, Pair of 1 or 9

  4. Little 2-4-6-8: A pung of 2,4,6,8, Pair of 2 or 8

  5. A Hash: A pung in each suit, any number, Pair and Pung of Opposite Honors
