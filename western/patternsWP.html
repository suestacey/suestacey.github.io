Wright Patterson Patterns

  1. Angels: 1s and 9s in each suit with Seven Odd Honors, any Terminal or Honor paired

  2. Asian Hash: Three different numbers punged in each suit,and pung and pair of Opposite Honors

  3. Betty: Three pairs in one suit (no Terminals) with Seven Odd Honors, one Honor paired

  4. Big 1-5-7-9 Hand: 1,5,7,9 and 1,5,7,9 in two suits with a Pung and 3 Singles, pair of Winds. Cannot Pung same number in both suits

  5. Big 2-4-6-8 Hand: 2,4,6,8 and 2,4,6,8 in two suits with a Pung and 3 Singles, pair of Winds. Cannot Pung same number in both suits.

  6. Big Chows: Chows of 7-8-9 in each suit, one chow of 7-8-9 in Mixed Suits, and a pair of 6s

  7. Chop Suey: Chows of 1-2-3 in each suit with NEWS and one Wind paired

  8. Chopsticks: 4 chows in one suit, pair of Corresponding Dragons

  9. Confused Chows (three suits): 1-2-3 in one suit, 4-5-6 in second suit, 7-8-9 in third suit with a pung and pair of Opposite Honors

  10. Confused Gates (three suits): 111 in one suit, 999 in second suit, and sequence of 2 to 8 in third suit with one tile paired

  11. Chinese New Year (any one suit): Pungs of 3s, 5s, 7s, pung of Corresponding Dragons, and one of each remaining Dragon

  12. Chow Mein: Chows of 7-8-9 in each suit with NEWS and one Wind paired

  13. Christmas Tree (any one suit); Four pungs and one pair of 1s, 5s, 7s, 9s, Red and/or Green Dragons (any combination)

  14. Dazzle: 5 Pairs in 1 suit, GWR with 1 paired

  15. Double Numbers: Two pungs of the same numbers in two suits with a pair of Winds or Dragons

  16. Doublets: Seven pairs in one suit 

  17. Dragon Line-up: One suit, 1 to 9 , one number paired from 2 to 8, 1 Each Dragon with 1 paired

  18. Dragon Gates (one suit and Corresponding Dragon): 111 or 999, with sequence of 2 to 8 (one tile paired), and a pung of Corresponding Dragons

  19. Dragon Snooze: Pungs of each Dragon with NEWS and one Wind paired

  20. Dragon's Breath: Pungs of each Dragon, pung of 1 Cracks, pair of East Winds

  21. Dragongale: Four pungs and a pair of Winds and Dragons in any combination

  22. Dragonwyck: 3 pungs of Dragons, and one pung of Own or Prevailing Wind and pair any other Wind

  23. Eleanor: Three different numbers punged in each suit,pung of East Winds, and pair of Red Dragons

  24. Emerald (Bamboos): Four pungs and/or chows and a pair, all in 3s, 4s, 5s, 7s, 9s, and/or Green Dragons

  25. Firecrackers: Three different numbers punged, 1 per suite, 1 Each Dragon with two paired

  26. 4th of July: Pairs of 4s and 7s in each suit, pair of Red Dragons

  27. Garnet (Characters): Four pungs and/or chows and a pair, all in 3s, 4s, 5s, 7s, 9s, and/or Red Dragons

  28. Grand Terminals: Four pungs and a pair of Terminals

  29. Heavenly Gates (one suit): 111 and 999, with sequence of 2 to 8 with one tile paired

  30. Heavenly Hash: Four pungs and a pair of Honors and/or Terminals

  31. Heavenly Hopscotch: 1-3-5-7-9 in two suits, and 2-4-6-8 in third suit

  32. Heavenly Twins: Not Available

  33. Hi-Jacks: Four concealed pungs and a pair all in one suit

  34. Honorable Hash: 1s and 9s in each suit with 4 pairs of Winds and/or Dragons

  35. Honorable Twins: Seven pairs of Winds and Dragons

  36. Honorettes: Five pairs of Honors and/or Terminals with NEWS

  37. Hopscotch: 1-3-5, 1-3-5-7, 1-3-5-7-9 in Mixed Suits with a pair of Winds or Dragon

  38. Idiots: Four like tiles in Mixed Suits (no duplicates) with a pair of Winds or Dragons

  39. Independence: 1776 in Mixed Suits and a pair of 4s

  40. Jade (Bamboos): Four pungs and/or chows and a pair, all in 2s, 3s, 4s, 6s, 8s, and/or Green Dragons

  41. Kong-Pung: Sequence of 1 to 9 in one suit with a kong and a pung (from 2 to 8)

  42. Lewis and Clark: 1804 in one suit, 1806 in second suit, NEWS with pung of Prevailing Wind

  43. Little 1-5-7-9 (two or three suits): One pung each of 1s, 5s, 7s, and 9s, with a pair of either 1s, or 9s

  44. Little 2-4-6-8 Hand (two or three suits): One pung each of2s, 4s, 6s, 8s, with a pair of either 2s or 8s

  45. Little Chows: Chows of 1-2-3 in each suit, one chow of 1-2-3 in Mixed Suits, and a pair of 4s

  46. Little Gates (three suits): Sequence of 1 to 9 in one suit, one tile paired from 2 to 8, and 1 and 9 in each of the other two suits

  47. Mars Pathfinder Landing: 7-4-1997 in two suits with a pair of Red Dragons

  48. May the "Fours" be with you: Pairs of 4s in each suit, Seven Odd Honors, one paired

  49. Middle Chows: Chows of 4-5-6 in each suit, one chow of 4-5-6 in Mixed Suits, and a pair of 3s

  50. Mish Mash: Not Available

  51. Neutrality: Three different numbers punged in each suit,pung of North Winds, and pair of 5 Bamboos

  52. News Line-up: Sequence of 1 to 9 in one suit,NEWS and one Wind paired

  53. Newsy Pungs: Three different numbers punged in Mixed Suits,  NEWS and one Wind paired

  54. Newsy Terminals: 1s and 9s in each suit with NEWS, each Wind paired

  55. Ohio Snow Suit: Sequence of 1 to 9 in one suit with a pair from 2 to 8 and SNOW

  56. Opal (Dots): Four pungs and/or chows and a pair, all in 3s, 4s, 5s, 7s, 9s, and/or White Dragons

  57. Open Gates (two suits): 111 and sequence of 2 to 5 in one suit, sequence of 5 to 8 and 999 in second suit

  58. Pearl (Dots): Four pungs or chows and a pair, all in 2s, 3s, 4s, 6s, 8s, or White Dragons

  59. Piano Keys: Sequence of 1 to 9 in one suit with two pungs and a pair (from 2 to 8)

  60. Picket Fence: 1-2-3-4-5 in one suit, 6-7-8-9 in second suit, NEWS and one Wind paired

  61. Poppers: Three different numbers punged in one suit, pung of Corresponding Dragons and pair of Winds

  62. Pung Chow: 1 suit, four chows or pungs and a pair

  63. Pungy News: Three different numbers punged in one suit, NEWS and one Wind paired

  64. Raindrops: Pairs of 2-4-6-8 in Dots, each Dragon paired

  65. Razzle: Five pairs in one suit with NEWS

  66. Repression: Sequence of 8 in a suit with 3 pairs of Winds or Dragons

  67. Royal Wedding: 4-29-11 in two suits, Red and White Dragons paired

  68. Ruby (Characters): Four pungs and/or chows and a pair, all in 2s, 3s, 4s, 6s, 8s, and/or Red Dragons

  69. Scotch-hopper: 2-4-6-8 in two suits, 1-3-5-7-9 in third suit with 1 or 9 paired

  70. Scotch-hopping: 2-4-6, 4-6-8, 2-4-6-8 in Mixed Suits with two pairs of any Winds and/or any Dragons

  71. Shamrock: Six pairs of Bams, pair of Green Dragons

  72. Snowflake: Six pairs of Dots, pair of White Dragons

  73. Sukiyaki: Chows of 4-5-6 in each suit, with NEWS and one Wind paired

  74. Susie Q: Three different numbers punged in each suit, pung of South Winds and pair of Green Dragons

  75. Termadrags: Five pairs of Terminals with Red, White, and Green Dragons, one Dragon paired

  76. Terminews: Three pungs of Terminals with NEWS and one Wind paired

  77. Termination: Four pungs of Terminals and a pair of Winds or Dragons

  78. Terms of Honors: Three pairs of Terminals and Seven Odd Honors, one Honor paired

  79. Trade Winds: Four pungs of Winds and Dragons and a pair of Terminals

  80. Tri-Numbers: Pungs of the same number in each suit, pung and pair of Winds or pung and pair of Dragons

  81. Valentine: Six pairs of Cracks, pair of Red Dragons

  82. Wally: Three different numbers punged in each suit, pung of West Winds, and pair of White Dragons

  83. Windy Pairs: Six pairs in any one suit, pair of Own or Prevailing Wind

  84. Windy Gates (one suit): 111 with a sequence 2 to 9, or 999 with sequence 1 to 8, with a pung of Own or Prevailing Wind

  85. Wright-Patt Wind Tunnel: Pungs of 1s and 9s in one suit with two pungs and pair of Winds
