He said, "Two will rest on a bed, the one will die, and the other will live."

Salome said, "Who are you, man, that you, a mortal being, have come upon my couch and eaten from my table?"

He said to her, "I am he who exists from the undivided. I was given some of the things of my Father"

... "I am your disciple."

... "Therefore I say, if he is destroyed be will be filled with Light, but if he is divided, he will be filled with Darkness."
