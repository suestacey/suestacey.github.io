30 Scorpio

A MASKED WITCH HOLDING A WAND OVER A GRINNING JACK-O- LANTERN

Tricky, shifting ground of apparent reality. Manipulating semblances to subtly change reality perceptions (misrepresentations; immersion in illusion). Magnetism, cunning, and charm. Realization that the insistence of our desires blinds us to real possibilities. Or rejected impulses that continue a furtive underworld existence. Occultism and magic.
