ATOP A MESA CROWDED WITH CAMERA-TOTING TOURISTS, A SHAMAN PERFORMS A MAJESTIC RITUAL TO THE SUN

Guarding the spiritual integrity of society from cultural invaders vs. establishing one's spiritual legitimacy in foreign territory (moving with authority and power; taking pride in one's cultural roots; secret powwows aimed at usurping power). Being called upon to act as a priest or priestess. Finding one's power spot.
