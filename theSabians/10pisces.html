10 Pisces

AVIATOR SAILS ACROSS THE SKY, MASTER OF THE HIGH REALMS

Olympian overview. The reflective mind (the gnostic Sophia), which cloaks the thoughts of god in image and form. Materializing one's artistic or social visions. Transforming one's environment into a castle of horrors or a celestial palace, according to whether love or selfishness rule the personality. Natural hierarchy. Use or misuse of knowledge and power. Questions of allegiance. Channeling prophetic wisdom (emotional distortions). Self-fulfilling prophecies: finding confirmations of one's hopes or fears. Attunement and mental clarity bred of emotional serenity. Taking one's place in the cosmic order. Giddy imaginings, or restoring perspective through humorous interjection.
