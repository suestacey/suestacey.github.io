3 Gemini

A FORMAL GARDEN CLOSELY SURROUNDED BY WILDERNESS. WALKING WITHIN, A BEAUTIFUL WOMAN WEARING A DIAMOND TIARA.

Spiritual aristocracy. Self-mastery. The rule of intelligence, reason, and refined culture over the dark and untamed forces of nature. Persephone: holding one's head up in the underworld. Establishing order in a wild or chaotic environment (fatal attraction to the underworld: crime, drugs, the sexual demi-monde). Exploring as yet uncharted territory. Cowboys and frontier women. Heroism and adventure.
