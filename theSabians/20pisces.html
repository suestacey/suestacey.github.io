SUNDOWN: RETURNING FROM WAR, A BOY APPROACHES THE OLD HOMESTEAD.

Prodigal son theme. Learning the true value of things through experience. Reclaiming one's spiritual or familial heritage. Losing oneself in the passions and concerns of everyday life vs. finding oneself. Major turning points where one's life comes into focus. Beginning a new cycle vs going in circles. Second chances and comebacks. Reaping what you sow. Abandoning true values for short-term gains, vs. reaping the fruits of a life well-lived. Falling in with good or bad associates. Harvesting life's deeper meanings. Attunement vs. falling out of harmony with oneself and out of grace with others. Symphonic harmonies. Celebrations of thanksgiving. Family values.
