11 Capricorn

A PHEASANT LEADS A FOX AWAY FROM ITS NEST WITH A FLASH OF BRILLIANT PLUMAGE

Keen perceptivity. Ability to penetrate surface appearances. Progressive and systematic distillation of experience. Literature (exotic or surreal fiction). Creating a verbal veil which both reveals and conceals the writer's inner world. Progressively uncovering a secret. Significant details. Seductive sensuality: courtship games. Clear sense of emotional loyalties (coquetry).
