12 Virgo

A GIRL WEARING A WREATH OF WILDFLOWERS LAUGHS AND OFFERS A FLOWER TO HER BOYFRIEND

Unaffected natural beauty. Sense of inner completion, readiness to reach out. Being honest and up-front about one's thoughts and feelings. Unclouded confidence that comes from a pure and affectionate heart (self-doubt, shy withdrawal, difficulty in expressing one's more refined emotions).
