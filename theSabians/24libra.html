24 Libra

A BUTTERFLY, WITH AN EXTRA LEFT WING, BALANCING ON A WREATH OF BABY'S BREATH

Mutation. Creative freedom. Thoughtless and effortless creation. Harmonious adaptability. Graceful harmony and interdependence of all the flora and fauna of the ecosphere (anima mundi). The imperfect mirroring or reflection of ideal forms by the real forms of nature. Evolution towards the ideal. The muse Terpsichore (dance; Venus).
