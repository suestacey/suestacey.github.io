28 Libra

A MAN IN DEEP GLOOM. UNNOTICED, A CIRCLE OF GLOWING ANGELS DESCENDS AROUND HIM


Spiritual disillusionment and morbid depression, which is dispersed by a healing spiritual illumination. Incorruptibility of pure spirit. The cloud's silver lining. Compassion for the human condition (tragic posing). The muse Melpomene (tragedy; Saturn).
