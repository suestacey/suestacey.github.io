23 Libra

A ROOSTER ON A WEATHER VANE, CROWING. IN THE BACKGROUND, THE RISING SUN.

Expanding one's intellectual scope. Clarity of perception (exaggeration). Seeking prominence through one's gifts. Effective self-promotion. Pomposity and self-importance, or self-deflating wit. The wisdom of foolishness and foolishness of wisdom. The muse Euphrosyne (mirth; Sun).
