Queen of Cups

Spirituality, maturity, and grace. A natural counselor and healer, One whose relaxed presence seems to embody deep love and spirituality. A tranquil poet who reflects the nature of the observer. The embrace of all things dreamlike and receptive, such as perfect and unconditional love.

Reverse

A melodramatic scene stealer. A sentimental hypochondriac. A person prey to wild and shifting emotional fancies.
