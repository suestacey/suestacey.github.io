Tower

Unforseen change. Danger, crisis, destruction, liberation, misery, distress, indigence, adversity, calamity, disgrace, deception, ruin.

Reverse

Unexpected upheaval leading to a positive change in life. Catastrophe survived or narrowly avoided. A new lifestyle and enlightenment. May indicate a broken relationship, divorce, or failure in business or career, negligence, absence, carelessness, distraction, apathy, vanity.
