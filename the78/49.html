8 of Discs

A time of hard work, commitment, diligence and dedication. The effort you put in will not be in vain. Frankness, honesty and innocence. Candid confrontation of circumstances. Putting your nose to the grindstone and doing the nitty gritty bits, and doing them even when you're bored or scared.

Reverse

Lack of ambition. Vanity. Conceit. Disillusionment. Usury. Hypocrisy. Flattery. Intrigue. Mismanagement of your workload or time. Failing to get a grip on the situation. Refusal to listen to a mentor or inner guide. A need for physical assessment and self-improvement. Seek appropriate education and training. Inability to take one step at a time. Lack of due diligence. Collapsing under a mountain of work.
