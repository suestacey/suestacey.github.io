Mah Jongg Pattern Assistant Icons

  1. Sort Flower to Joker
    The tiles in the rack and the wall are sorted as follows: flowers, 1-9 bams, 1-9 dots, 1-9 craks, winds as N-E-W-S, dragons green, white, red and jokers.
  2. Sort the Wall
    Sorts the tiles, in the wall, numerically as follows: flowers, 1 bam, 1 dot, 1 crak, 2 bam, 2 dot, 2 crak etc, winds, dragons, jokers.
  3. Sort the Rack
    Sorts the tiles, in the rack, numerically as follows: flowers, 1 bam, 1 dot, 1 crak, 2 bam, 2 dot, 2 crak etc, winds, dragons, jokers.
  4. Sort by Suit
    The tiles in the rack and the wall are sorted as flowers, 1-9 bams, green dragons, 1-9 dots, white dragons, 1-9 craks, red dragons, winds, jokers.
  5. Check the Pattern
    Compare the tiles in the rack against the system-stored patterns to see if its a valid pattern. Shows the first pattern found.
  6. Check the Pattern
    Compare the tiles in the rack against the system-stored patterns to see if its a valid pattern. Show all the patterns found.

  7. Add the tile in the discard section back into the rack.
  8. Pattern List
    Show the list of all current valid patterns.
  9. Sort Descending
    Sorts the tiles, in the rack, from Joker to the Flower.

  10. Clear the tile in the discard section.
