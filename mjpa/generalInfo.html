The definitive app for practicing your patterns !

If it's not possible for you to get to a Mah Jongg game RIGHT NOW, then this app will keep your mind fresh and focused on all the 2020 patterns.

Gives you the piece of mind to practice those patterns without your sharky, or not so sharky, opponents hollering "That's It, Mah Jongg" in your ear every 10 minutes.

Last year I created this app to help me learn the 2019 patterns quickly. I decided to share it with the rest of the community for 2020.
