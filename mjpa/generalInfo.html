The definitive app for practicing your patterns !

If it's not possible for you to get to a Mah Jongg game RIGHT NOW, then this app will keep your mind fresh and focused on all the 2025 patterns.

Gives you the piece of mind to practice those patterns without your sharky, quirky, or not so snarky, opponents hollering "That's It, Mah Jongg" in your ear every 10 minutes.

I created this app, in 2019, to help me learn the patterns quickly. I decided to share it with the rest of the community in 2020.

The system also includes the ability to play the 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023 and 2024 patterns.
